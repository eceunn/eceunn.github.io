ECE 574 Course Outline

  1. Introduction to discrete and continuous signals: shifting, scaling, delay and compression
  2. System connection: series, parallel, series-parallel, feedback
  3. Linear and Time invariant systems
  4. System decomposition: step, impulse and Fourier decomposition
  5. Convolution of signals
  6. Stability of systems
  7. Z-transforms of systems: Poles and Zeros
  8. Concepts of signal sampling
  9. Discrete Fourier series and Discrete Fourier Transform
  10. Concept of Digital filtering: types of filters and their applications
  11. Synthesis of low pass filters
